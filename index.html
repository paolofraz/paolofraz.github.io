Paolo Frazzetto

Ph.D. Candidate · University of Padova · paolo.frazzetto@phd.unipd.it

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Brain, Mind & Computer Science Doctoral School at the University of Padova, Italy.
My thesis was accepted and I plan to graduate in March 2025.

Currently I am working in the SymboliG project within the Future of Artificial Intelligence (FAIR) group, where I am researching how to apply symbolic and domain knowledge for Generative AI. During the Ph.D., I worked in AI Applications with Human Resources Data at Amajor SB S.p.a, under the supervision of Prof. Alessandro Sperduti. I additionaly spent a six month visiting experience in Bielefeld, Germany, at the HammerLab. I have a broad interest in Statistical and Machine Learning, with a focus on techniques for structured data, and in particular Graph Neural Networks and Deepl Learning applied to natural and social sciences.

I regularly supervise thesis of MSc in Data & Computer Science, and I am open to external collaborations. You are welcome to reach out to me!
