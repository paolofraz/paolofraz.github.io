Paolo Frazzetto

PostDoc in Machine Learning · University of Padova · paolo.frazzetto@unipd.it

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Machine Learning Group of the University of Padova, Italy. Currently I am working in the SymboliG project within the Future of Artificial Intelligence (FAIR) group, where I am researching how to apply symbolic and domain knowledge for Generative AI.

During the PhD, I worked in AI Applications with Human Resources Data at Amajor SB S.p.a, under the supervision of Prof. Alessandro Sperduti within the Brain, Mind & Computer Science Doctoral School at the University of Padova.
I additionaly spent six month in Bielefeld, Germany, visitng the HammerLab. I have a broad interest in Statistical and Machine Learning, with a focus on techniques for structured data, and in particular Graph Neural Networks and Deepl Learning applied to natural and social sciences.

I am eager to advance my career in research, with a particular focus in postdoctoral and research scientist opportunities. I am actively seeking new positions, and I am available to relocate globally from September/October 2025.

Best way to reach out to me is via e-mail.
